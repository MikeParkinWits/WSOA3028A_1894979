About Me

The Man Behind the Camera

Hi, I'm Jake Sky! Most of you probably don't know me, but that's alright because hopefully you will soon! I'm originally from Texas, although I currently live in sunny California! I am obsessed with all things photography and filmmaking, and I've been lucky enough to make my obsession my work! At the moment I'm a freelance photographer, and I'm using this website to showcase my skills and portfolio - so if you're looking for a photographer, why not get in touch?

Oh I forgot, if you're wondering what I look like - that's me there on the left! I know I look scary, but if you ever see me in person, come say hi - but just know that I always have my camera with me, so you might get a spontaneous photoshoot. And if you want to keep in the loop about where I am and what I've been up to, why not give me a follow on one of my social medias? It's as easy as clicking the icons under my picture!

Anyway, that's me for now - Stay Motivated, Stay Inspired, and Never Stop Creating.
