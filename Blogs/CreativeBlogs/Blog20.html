The Search Begins

By Jake Sky | 6 June 2021 | Words: 353 | Creative

Welcome back! So, last time we ended off with lockdown restrictions easing up in South Africa, allowing us to finally leave the Airbnb a bit more often. We took this opportunity with both hands, as we didn’t know how long this would last, and we thought it would be best to start visiting the different places we noted on our list. Unfortunately, we quickly learnt that while we were allowed out more easily now, a lot of the locations we wanted to visit were still closed… But not all!

Out of the list of places open to the public, it just so happened that one of the potential locations for our opening shot was allowed to continue operations (as it was extremely Covid friendly and outdoors). It was a sculpture park, in this place Krugersdorp, called NIROX. But there was one problem… Because things were still officially in lockdown, we couldn’t rent a car – and all the Ubers seemed to be restricted too… Luckily for us, our gracious Airbnb hosts – who, if you remember, let us stay even though they were not officially allowed to be open – had a spare car which they let us use. So, off we went to NIROX Sculpture Park.

So, with our first location found, we all started to feel better about how this new film would turn out – and we were excited to keep looking at locations around the city. But that’s for another day… But if you want to visualize what I’ve been talking about, why not check out my portfolio page where I’ve uploaded some photos of the park?

Until next time - Stay Motivated, Stay Inspired, and Never Stop Creating.
