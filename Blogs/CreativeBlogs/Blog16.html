What Next?

By Jake Sky | May 14 2021 | Words: 353 | Creative

So, where did we end off? Ah yes, we had just gotten the terrible news that we were now stuck in our layover city of Johannesburg, South Africa. At the time, I don’t know what we were more scared of – being stuck in a foreign country with no plans, the coronavirus outbreak, or the fact that our dreams of filming a movie in Zambia now seemed impossible. Realistically, it was most likely a combination of all three…

We searched for any way to get to Zambia, but that seemed null and void at that point. So, with reality now setting in, the only thing we could do was look for accommodation. Lucky for us we managed to book an Airbnb where the owners let us stay for the duration of the lockdown, even though all forms of accommodation were officially closed soon after we arrived. We spent a few days moping around, thinking that our dream of making this movie had gone down the drain, but then I had a revelation. What if we filmed the entire movie in South Africa as opposed to Zambia? I ran the idea past the others, and while we agreed the situation wasn’t ideal, we knew that it was our only chance of getting this movie made. Besides, we were stuck in a small Airbnb for, at that point, an unknown period – we had nothing better to do than try and rewrite the script for South Africa.

So, we got to work and soon realized that not all was lost and that what we were trying to pull off might just work. Unfortunately, while we were able to do a lot of research about places to film, it was challenging to get an idea of the place since we couldn’t actually visit there. But, soon enough, the lockdown started to ease down a bit more, and that’s when things really got started…

But that’s where I’ll leave it for now – so come back soon to hear more about our adventures and roadblocks along the way!
