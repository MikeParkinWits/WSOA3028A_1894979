Exploring the City

By Jake Sky | 11 June 2021 | Words: 338 | Creative

With our first location now found, NIROX Sculpture Park, we all started to have the realization that maybe our crazy idea would work after all. So, after a successful first day of location scouting, we all headed back to the Airbnb and celebrated – although we would have preferred a restaurant, but that was still a thing of the past at the time.

The next day we woke up bright and early – excited to find our next location. Unlike in my last blog, this time I won’t go into as much detail about each location so that I don’t spoil the entire plot of the movie! Instead, I’ll tell you broadly about the areas we explored. Next, we needed to find where most of our movie would be set. To do this, we explored some more areas around the city. While this was a bit challenging, because of the lockdown restrictions, we still managed to get an idea of where we wanted things to happen.

This part took a couple weeks, and we ended up exploring so many different areas – Germiston, Edenvale, and Braamfontein just to name a few – but on the final day, when we were getting a bit worn out, we explored two areas where we knew we wanted the film to be based: Sandton and Rosebank. Both were a mix of shopping malls (with both apparently holding some of the most popular ones in the country), business towers, and residential. These felt perfect to us as they were modern, had lots of different locations that could be used, and best of all is that they were quite close to our Airbnb so we could easily move between the two – allowing us to optimize our time while filming.

So now we could now check off our opening scene, and most of the other scenes in the movie however, there was still one more location we needed to find… But I’ll keep that for next time.

Until next time - Stay Motivated, Stay Inspired, and Never Stop Creating.
