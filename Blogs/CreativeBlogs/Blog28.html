The Final Location

By Jake Sky | 26 June 2021 | Words: 456 | Creative

Alright, welcome back to the sixth installment of my (almost) bi-monthly blog! Firstly, I’d like to thank you all for sticking around this long – it means a lot to me and the crew! And if you stick around just a bit longer, we’ll have some exciting news to share about the movie really soon! But enough of that, let’s get back to why you’re here…

So, last time you checked in we’d almost found all our filming spots in Johannesburg. However, I said there was one left… For the astute of you, I’m sure you’ve realized that I was talking about the end location! Now, I’m going to try and keep things as vague as possible, so I don’t spoil the movie before it’s even released, but keep in mind there will be minor spoilers ahead.

So, by now Casey, Luke, and I had searched all around Johannesburg and its surrounding areas (as much as we could during the lockdown), and we were struggling to find a good location for the end scene. Most things were either closed, or not right. However, we had gotten this far so we were not going to give up so easily. We’d almost crossed off all the spots on the list, but then our kind Airbnb hosts told us about a little spot named Maboneng in Johannesburg’s CBD. Now, they warned us that the CBD could be a little dangerous in places, but we were excited to go check it out nonetheless. However, just to be safe we unpacked our expensive camera equipment from the car and headed out – using our phones to take some test shots.

Although we were all a bit on edge, once we started walking around the feeling of nervousness turned into intrigue. There was so much going on in the CBD, and it felt extremely lively. After a few minutes trying to find our way, we then reached Maboneng – this neighborhood that Casey describes as South Africa’s version of Williamsburg in Brooklyn. Although a lot of restaurants were closed because of the lockdown, it was still full of energy. In fact, we found a local bar where the owner invited us in with open arms and it just felt right – we knew this is where we wanted to hold the end of the film. We all spoke about in briefly and asked the owner on the spot – and to our surprise he said yes!

So, with that, we officially had all our shooting locations in the books! We had now finished phase one of our crazy plan, but now onto phase 2… The actual filming. But I’ll leave that for the next blog.

Until next time - Stay Motivated, Stay Inspired, and Never Stop Creating.
