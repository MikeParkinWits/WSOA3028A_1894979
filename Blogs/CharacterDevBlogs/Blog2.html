Jake Sky Character Description

By Michael Parkin | March 12th 2021 | Character Development

Jake Sky had always stood out from a young age, if not for his height, then for his appearance. However, it is as if he had chosen to stand out for the last 10, of his 28, years as his appearance looked straight out of a modern-day version of Grease. He never left home without his trusty leather jacket and always made sure that his dirty blonde hair was slicked back, although a few strands always managed to fall forward onto his tanned skin as the day progressed. In the off chance that you would see Jake without his jacket on, one might notice his toned build and faint white tattoo on his left arm. Even though his appearance scared many off, those who were not scared off were pleasantly surprised once he took off his dark shaded sunglass and introduced himself with a charming smile, and dignified Californian accent.

Although originally born in Texas, Jake had moved around more than most during his childhood because of his father’s work. As a result, Jake became close with his family, and relied on his younger sister Kee for emotional support. However, when his father passed away, his mother decided to settle down in California so that Jake and Kee could have a more stable childhood. When moving to California, Jake came across his father’s old polaroid camera and remembered all the fond memories they had using it together before he passed. From that day on, Jake took the Polaroid everywhere he went and subsequently gained the same obsession for photography as his father.

But for Jake, this was more than just an obsession, and quickly became his career, sending him to all corners of the globe. Although he no longer kept his father’s Polaroid by his side, it would be a rare sight to see him without his beaten-up Canon glued to his hand. However, even though Jake had worked endlessly to gain recognition in the photography industry, he soon realized that he wanted to do more than just photography and explore filmmaking too. Though he had yet to successfully break into the film industry, he was willing to do anything to achieve his goal.

This passion led Jake to his final attempt at filmmaking. He knew that this would be his last attempt at filmmaking, so he risked it all and, along with his two best friends (Casey and Luke), wrote an independent film set in Zambia. However, while traveling to film and produce it they had a layover in Johannesburg where they had been hit with a new, unexpected, roadblock. Because of an outbreak of the coronavirus, South Africa had been put into lockdown and they were now stuck in Johannesburg indefinitely. Armed with this new problem, Jake and his friends decided to do the unthinkable and rewrite the entire film to be set in a city they knew nothing about – Johannesburg. While researching the city, Jake uses his photography skills to capture the city and gain insight for his film.
